About

Clay Crozier, Esq.

Clay Crozier was born and raised in Neola, Utah. He received his Associate degree in Animal Science from Colorado Northwestern Community College. He earned his Bachelor’s degree from Southern Utah University. He attained his Juris Doctor from the University of South Dakota School of Law.

Clay focuses his practice on estate planning, probate, property, and business law, serving clients throughout the Uinta Basin and across Utah.
